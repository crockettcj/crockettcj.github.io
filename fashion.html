Fashion Gallery

As you have probably already read, my first love and passion is Fashion. I love textiles and construction a like. I also enjoy illustration. Below are some collections I put together as well as some miscellaneous projects I put together while experimenting with couture techniques and draping.

Lapping Zen

This is a collection of yoga wear. The target audience is young women who actively participate in yoga and running. The following are 5 pages explaining and displaying this collection.

Laced Up

Laced Up is a line of formal wear that incorporates flowers and dance. The line was designed for women who are upper-middle class and need to attend one to many black tie events. The following are 5 pages explaining and displaying this collection. A special thanks goes out to my friend Nora for letting me use images of her as my target customer for this collection.

Classic Bows

Classic Bows is a line of business wear created for young professionals which integrates classic iconic styles with bows. The following are 5 pages explaining and displaying this collection. A special thanks goes out to my friend Parisa for letting me use images of her as my target customer for this project.
