About Me

The person behind the designs

Some Background Info

I'm Catherine, but most people call me Katie. I have a passion for designs of all kinds. My specialties are fashion, graphic and web. I am also a problem solver by nature. I think that's what I love design. I most recently received an Associate of Art Degree in Graphic and Web Design from the Art Institute of Washington, and I not-so-recently received a Bachelor of Science Degree in Fashion Design and Merchandising with minors in Business Adminstration and Advertising from West Virginia University.

As a recent graduate of The Art Institute of Washington, I discovered that I am not only a skilled graphic designer, but I also enjoy web design. Reading HTML, CSS and JavaScript makes me feel like I'm running the Matrix. I went into school thinking that I would come out a printmaker, and came out both a printmaker and a digital junkie. This is the 21st century after all!

As a not-so-recent graduate of West Virginia University, I have a Bachelors of Science in Fashion Design and Merchandising. While attending WVU, I learned the skill of long nights in a sewing studio, and a full appreciate of textiles and apparel construction. I also learned the valuable skills of being an avid sports fan. Let's go, Mountaineers!

What can I do for you? A lot, actually. I can make pretty clothes. I can make pretty prints. I can also make websites. If you need any or all of the above, feel free to reach out to me.
